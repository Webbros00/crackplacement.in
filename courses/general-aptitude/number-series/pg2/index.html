Number Series




Question 1.
121,144,169, ___, 400

Solution:
This sequence consists of square of consecutive numbers 11, 12, 13... and so on Hence 196 will come in the blank.

Question 2.
8,27,64, 125, —, 343

Solution:
This sequence comprises of a series of consecutive numbers with perfect cubes the is (2*2*2), (3*3*3), (4*4*4), …. and so on Hence 216 will be the number in the blank space.

Question 3.
3, 6, 9, 12, __, 18, 21

Solution:
Here the series follows a three-unit increment to each element to get the next element 3
6 (3+3)
9 (6+3)
12 (9+3)
15 (12+3) -> in the blank position
18 (15+3)
21 (18+3)


Question 4.
1, 2, 4, __, 16, 32

Solution:
Here a= 1 every next term is multiplied by a factor of 2 Hence, we get:
1
1*2 = 2
2*2 = 4
4*2 = 8, ….
